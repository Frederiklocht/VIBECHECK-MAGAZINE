UPCOMING - KODI

If you haven’t heard about KODI yet, then it’s time to get acquainted. KODI consist of the twin brothers, William and Frederik, from Aarhus, Denmark. They have been making music since 2018 but just started releasing music in June this year. Since then they’ve released 2 singles that have amassed over 26.000 streams combined without promotion. During the summer they also had shows at venues like Aarhus Volume and Cluster Square.

In an interview the brothers were asked about their biggest hopes and dreams, and the producer Will said:

It wouldn’t be surprising to see this happen with the way their numbers have been looking so far. And now that they have made a deal with the record label and publishing company, SUMO Entertainment, it’s looking good for the 23 year old brothers from Denmark.

To the ones that are eager to hear new music from KODI, they told us they are releasing their debut EP in the beginning of 2021 – so stay tuned.
